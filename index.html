Statement

On material, light, and the landscape of Kosovo.

Two point zero Architecture is a studio based in Pristina, working across residential, public, and commercial projects throughout Kosovo and the Balkans. We approach each commission as a conversation between material, light, and the particular landscape of a place.

“ Architecture is the will of an epoch translated into space. — a reminder, from Mies.

The studio is small by design. Each drawing passes through the same hands from first sketch to final detail. What we build is quiet, precise, and intended to settle slowly into its place — to age rather than expire.
